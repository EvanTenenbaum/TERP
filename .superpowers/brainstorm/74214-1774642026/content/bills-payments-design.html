Payments — Key Design Notes

  • No aging panel — Payments don't age. KPI badges show totals by direction (received vs sent).
  • Simpler filter — just All / Received / Sent (not a full status machine).
  • Invoice column is clickable — navigates to Invoices tab with ?invoiceId= deep-link.
  • Amount color-coded — green for received, red for sent. Instant visual direction.
  • Record Payment action — opens InvoiceToPaymentFlow golden flow (same sidecar as Invoices).
  • Void action — existing void confirmation dialog from pilot.
  • Deep-link support — ?id=, ?invoiceId=, ?orderId= from existing PaymentsPilotSurface.
