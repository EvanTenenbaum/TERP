Vendor AP Context — What It Shows

When you select a bill, the inspector shows this bill's details plus a vendor context card that answers: "How much do we owe this vendor total? What other bills are open?" This gives Evan the full picture without navigating away. The other open bills are clickable (selects that row in the grid).

Data comes from filtering the existing accounting.bills.list response by vendor ID — no new tRPC endpoint needed.
