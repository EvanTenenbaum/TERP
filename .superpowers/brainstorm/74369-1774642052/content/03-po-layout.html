Three Modes, One Component

Queue mode (default on Procurement workspace): Full PO queue grid + selected PO details below. "New PO" button transitions to creation mode.

Creation mode (triggered by "New PO" or "Edit" on a draft): Split surface — left is the multi-source product browser (Supplier History | Low Stock | Catalog), right is the editable PO document grid with invoice-bottom totals and terms.

Receiving mode (on Inventory workspace receiving tab): Split surface — left is filtered PO queue (CONFIRMED + RECEIVING only), right is selected PO's line items with receive-qty inputs and "Submit to Intake" handoff. Different users from PO creation — this is warehouse staff.

The mode prop gates which layout renders. Queue → Creation transition is URL-param driven (?poView=create or ?poId=X&poView=edit).
