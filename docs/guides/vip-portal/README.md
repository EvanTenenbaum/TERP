# VIP Portal User Guide

Welcome to the TERP VIP Portal. This guide is written for non-technical users and walks through logging in, browsing your dashboard, booking appointments, downloading documents, and managing notifications.

![VIP portal landing](./images/overview.png)

## What You Can Do

- Monitor your balances and orders from the dashboard
- Browse live catalog listings shared with you
- Submit purchase requests or supply offers
- Book appointments with your TERP account team
- Download invoices/bills and view transaction history
- Stay informed with in-app notifications
