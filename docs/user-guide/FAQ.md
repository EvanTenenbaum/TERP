# Frequently Asked Questions

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-31

---

## General

### How do I reset my password?

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact your administrator.

### How do I change my profile information?

1. Click your name/avatar in the top right
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Update your information
4. Click **"Save"**

### Why can't I access certain features?

Access is controlled by your user role and permissions. If you need access to a feature, contact your administrator to request the appropriate permissions.

### How do I log out?

Click your name/avatar in the top right and select **"Logout"**.

## Orders & Sales

### How do I create a new order?

1. Navigate to **Sales → Orders**
2. Click **"+ New Order"**
3. Select a customer (or create new)
4. Add products and quantities
5. Click **"Save"** or **"Confirm"**

### What's the difference between an Order and a Quote?

- **Quote:** A price estimate for the customer. Not committed.
- **Order:** A confirmed sale. Reserves inventory and enters fulfillment.

### How do I convert a quote to an order?

1. Open the quote
2. Click **"Convert to Order"**
3. Review details
4. Confirm

### Can I edit a confirmed order?

Limited edits are possible before picking begins. Major changes may require cancelling and recreating the order.

### How do I cancel an order?

1. Open the order
2. Click **"Cancel Order"**
3. Provide a reason
4. Confirm

Note: Cancellation releases reserved inventory.

## Inventory

### How do I add new inventory?

Use the **Intake** process:

1. Navigate to **Inventory → Intake**
2. Click **"+ New Intake"**
3. Enter vendor and items
4. Complete quality check
5. Finalize intake

### Why does available quantity differ from on-hand?

**On-hand** is the total physical quantity. **Available** is on-hand minus reservations (committed to orders).

### How do I adjust inventory quantities?

1. Navigate to **Inventory → Movements**
2. Click **"+ New Movement"**
3. Select **"Adjustment"**
4. Enter batch, quantity change, and reason
5. Submit (may require approval)

### What do the stock alerts mean?

- **Low Stock:** Below minimum threshold - consider reordering
- **Out of Stock:** Zero available - urgent attention needed
- **Expiring Soon:** Approaching expiration date

## Clients & CRM

### How do I add a new customer?

**Quick method (during order):**

1. In order form, click **"+ New Customer"**
2. Enter name and contact
3. Click **"Create"**

**Full method:**

1. Navigate to **Clients**
2. Click **"+ New Client"**
3. Fill in all details
4. Save

### How do I view a customer's purchase history?

1. Open the client profile
2. Go to **"History"** tab
3. See all past orders and transactions

### How do I apply a credit to a customer's order?

1. Create or edit an order
2. In payment section, click **"Apply Credit"**
3. Enter amount (up to available balance)
4. Save

### How do I see what a customer wants to buy?

Check the **Wishlist** tab on their profile. This shows products they've requested that may not be in stock.

## Accounting

### How do I record a customer payment?

**Quick method:**

1. From Dashboard, click **"Receive Payment"**
2. Select customer, enter amount
3. Choose payment type
4. Save

**From client profile:**

1. Open client profile
2. Click **"Receive Payment"**
3. Complete the form

### How do I pay a vendor?

1. From Dashboard, click **"Pay Vendor"**
2. Select vendor, enter amount
3. Choose payment type
4. Optionally apply to specific bills
5. Save

### How do I generate a receipt?

After recording a payment, click **"Download Receipt"** or **"Print Receipt"**.

From client profile, click **"Generate Tab Screenshot"** for current balance.

### What is a fiscal period?

A fiscal period is an accounting time frame (usually monthly). Transactions are recorded within periods for proper financial reporting.

## Pick & Pack

### How do I see orders ready for picking?

Navigate to **Pick & Pack**. The queue shows all orders with status **CONFIRMED** that need fulfillment.

### How do I pack items into bags?

1. Open an order in Pick & Pack
2. Check items to pack together
3. Click **"Pack Selected"**
4. Assign to new or existing bag
5. Confirm

### Why can't I mark an order as ready?

All items must be packed before marking ready. Check for unpacked items.

### How do I print a packing slip?

1. Open the order in Pick & Pack
2. Click **"Print Packing Slip"**

## Reports & Analytics

### How do I access the leaderboard?

Navigate to **Analytics → Leaderboard**. You can filter by time period, client type, and metrics.

### How do I export data?

Most list views have an **"Export"** button. Click it and choose:

- CSV for spreadsheets
- PDF for reports
- JSON for data integration

### How do I see my sales performance?

1. Navigate to **Analytics → Leaderboard**
2. Find your ranking
3. Click your name for detailed metrics

## Technical Issues

### The page won't load

1. Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if issue persists

### I'm seeing an error message

1. Note the exact error message
2. Try the action again
3. If it persists, contact support with:
   - Error message
   - What you were trying to do
   - Time it occurred

### The system is slow

1. Check your internet connection
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Report to support if ongoing

### I can't find something

Use the **global search** (press `/` or click search bar) to search across:

- Clients
- Orders
- Products
- Quotes

## Security

### How is my data protected?

- All connections use HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are securely hashed
- Sessions expire after inactivity
- Access is controlled by permissions

### What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

1. Change your password immediately
2. Log out of all sessions
3. Contact your administrator
4. Report any suspicious activity
